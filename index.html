About Steve Davis

Your Notary. At Your Location. On Your Schedule.

I'm Steve Davis, a California Commissioned Notary Public serving Glendale, Burbank, Pasadena, and the greater Los Angeles area. After building a career in operations where precision and attention to detail aren't optional, I found that becoming a Notary Public was a natural fit — a way to put those skills to work while doing something I genuinely love: helping people.

A notary is often called during life's most significant moments — signing a power of attorney for an aging parent, finalizing a trust, or navigating important legal paperwork. I take that responsibility seriously. Whether it's one of your best days or one of your most difficult ones, I'm honored to be there and to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

I come to your home, office, hospital room, care facility, or any location that's convenient for you — because the last thing you should have to worry about is finding a notary.
