Before Your Appointment

What to Have Ready

  • A valid, government-issued photo ID — driver's license, passport, or state ID. Must not be expired.
  • Your document — whether to sign it before I arrive depends on the type of notarization. For a jurat (affidavit or sworn statement), you must sign in my presence. For an acknowledgment (the most common type), you may have already signed. When in doubt, wait and I'll guide you through it.
  • Any required witnesses — some documents (advance health care directives, certain trusts) require witnesses. Arrange for them to be present; I cannot serve as a witness.
  • Payment ready — cash, Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, or check.

Not sure what to prepare? Check the FAQ or just reach out — I'm happy to walk you through it.
