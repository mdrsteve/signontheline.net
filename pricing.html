Pricing

Transparent, flat-rate fees — no surprises

I believe you should know exactly what you're paying before I arrive. All fees are listed below. Your total is the notarization fee plus your travel zone, plus any applicable surcharges — no surprises.

Notarization

Signature Fee

California sets the maximum notarization fee by law. I charge the state maximum.

Service Fee
Notarized signature (acknowledgment or jurat) $15 per signature
Oath or affirmation (no notarial certificate) $15 per person
Certified copy of power of attorney $15 per copy
Multiple signatures: Many documents require more than one notarized signature. Each signature on each separate document counts as one notarization. For example, if a power of attorney and a grant deed each need one signature notarized, that's two fees of $15 each.

Travel Fees

Service Zones

Travel is charged as a flat fee by zone — no per-mile calculations, no guesswork. Your zone is based on your city.

Zone Cities Included Travel Fee
Zone 1 Glendale, Burbank, La Canada Flintridge, La Crescenta, Montrose $35
Zone 2 Pasadena, Arcadia, Monrovia, North Hollywood, Studio City, Toluca Lake $50
Zone 3 Sherman Oaks, Van Nuys, Northridge, Alhambra, Downtown LA, Silver Lake $65
Zone 4 Areas beyond Zone 3 (Woodland Hills, Pomona, Long Beach, etc.) By quote

Not sure which zone you're in? Send me a message with your city and I'll confirm your travel fee before you book.

Additional Fees

Surcharges & Add-Ons

The following surcharges apply when relevant and stack with each other where applicable.

Surcharge When It Applies Fee
After-Hours Mon–Sat 7pm–9pm, or any time Sunday +$35
Rush / Same-Day Appointments booked with less than 2 hours notice during standard hours +$35
Hospital / Care Facility Any signing at a hospital, nursing home, or care facility +$25
Waiting Fee After 20 minutes of wait time beyond scheduled start $2/minute
Printing Documents printed and brought to appointment (2-hour advance notice required) $1/page
Scanning Documents scanned at appointment $1/page
Journal Record Copies Copies of notarial journal entries (written request required per CA Gov. Code §8206) $0.30/page
Surcharges stack. For example, a rush appointment after 7pm would incur both the rush surcharge (+$35) and the after-hours surcharge (+$35), for a combined +$70 above the base fees.
